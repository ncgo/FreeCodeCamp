Interviewing

A technical interview is your chance to show you have the skills to match.

They want to see how you think, not just that you can.

Also, they want to see that you care about the quality of the product that you ship and the experience customers have.

Brush up on the basics beforehand. Practice your coding, design and algorithms, and always validate or test your solutions.

  2. Your ability to innovate

  4. How you adapt to changing requirements
  5. Your problem-solving approach
  6. Ypur analytical thinking skills
  7. How you handle feedback
